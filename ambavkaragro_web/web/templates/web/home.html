    About Us

    Ambavkar Agro

    was founded in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra and brings high quality agricultural inputs for Indian farmers across Domestic & International Market.

    Founded as a private company in 2017 which became a publicly traded company in 2022, Over the years we have diversified our operations into various sectors like Alpanso Mango, Kesar Mango, Banana, Onion, Custard Apple, Guava, trading of Agri commodities & Retailing.

    we derive a competitive advantage in the domestic and International Market. We have developed product expertise and built enduring relationships with our business partners in India and abroad. We have always maintained a vision of being the leader in providing products that are environmentally sustainable and work in conjuncture with nature to help farmers be the best stewards of their lands.

    We are suppliers to leading institutions and global retail chains. Our prime focus lies towards timely execution of contracts and keeping our commitments to the satisfaction of our business partners.

    We have a dedicated team of engineers whose passion is to create products inspired from nature that can help farmers to grow better crops while using natural fertilizers. Our goal is to help reduce pesticide and chemical fertilize use while improving yields and quality of crops for a healthy tomorrow.

    The growing demand for food and well-being on the part of the world's population requires an effective response. Relying on a return to nature is not a realistic alternative because it does not suffice to meet global needs. On the other hand, excessive use of chemical products implies a non-sustainable choice in the long term from the environmental point of view.
